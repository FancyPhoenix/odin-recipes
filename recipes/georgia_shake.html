Georgia Shake Recipe

This is a recipe for a chocolate milk shake
called Georgia shake

Ingredients

  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 chocolate covered peanut butter cup, crumbled
  • ¾ cup chocolate ice cream

Recipe Steps

  1. In a blender, combine milk, peanut butter cup and ice cream. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup and sprinkle some chocolate crumps and enjoy!
